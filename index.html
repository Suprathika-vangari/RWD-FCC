# tribute-page- Tribute to the legend APJ Abdul Kalam

APJ ABDUL KALAM

Missile man of India

Former president of India

APJ Abdul kalam was known for his generosity. He was the 11th president of India and a pioneer of the country's space and missile programmes, passed away on July 27,2015. He lived an unpretentious lifestyle.

Here is a timeline of his life...

  • 1931 - Avul Parikar Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on October 15 in Rameswaram,TamilNadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from Saint Joseph's College.
  • 1960 - Graduated in Aerospace Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1961 - Joined DRDO as a Scientist.
  • 1969 - Joined Space Research centre at ISRO.
  • 1980 - Became the project director for India's First Indegenous Satellite Program.
  • 1980 - Was involved in the development of several Indigenous Missiles for India like Agni and Prithvi.
  • 1980 - First indigenous satellite launch vehicle(SLV-III),developed under the stewardship of Dr.Kalam
  • 1981 - Awarded Padma Bhushan.
  • 1990 - Held as Cheif advisor for the India's Nuclear program.
  • 1992 - Awarded Bharat Ratna
  • 1999 - Principal Scientific advisor to the Government of India.
  • 2002 - Became the 11th President of India.
  • 2015 - The Missile Man of India Dr.APJ Abdul kalam passes away....
